Our Story

Built on Experience.
Driven by Results.

Crown Construction Management was founded on a simple belief: every property owner deserves a dedicated advocate who puts their interests first. Too often, owners navigate complex construction projects without the expertise, oversight, or representation they need — and they pay the price in cost overruns, schedule delays, and compromised quality.

We exist to change that. Crown provides senior-level construction management and owner's representation services that give owners the confidence to build. Whether you're developing a ground-up project, renovating an investment property, or navigating a parcel split, we bring the discipline, transparency, and hands-on oversight your project demands.

Our team brings deep experience across residential and commercial projects throughout the Bay Area, Greater Sacramento, and Northern California. We know the local codes, the regulatory landscape, and the contractor market — and we use that knowledge to protect your investment at every stage.
