What We Do

Our Services

Expert construction management services designed to protect your investment and deliver your project on time, on budget, and with full transparency at every stage.

Full Service Offerings

Comprehensive Project Oversight

From feasibility through closeout, Crown Construction Management provides the expertise and oversight your project demands.

01

Construction Management

Comprehensive oversight of your project from the initial planning phase through construction completion. We manage the entire process to ensure your goals for quality, cost, and timeline are met — so you can focus on what matters.

02

Owner's Representation

Acting on your behalf to protect your interests at every stage of the project. We manage contractors, review budgets, negotiate change orders, and ensure decisions are made in your best interest — not the builder's.

03

Pre-Construction Planning

Feasibility studies, conceptual budgeting, contractor vetting, and strategy development before a single dollar is spent on construction. We front-load the intelligence your project needs to eliminate costly surprises.

04

Parcel Split Management

Expert coordination and guidance through subdivision and entitlement processes. We manage the complexity of parcel splits to maximize your land value and development potential.

05

Design & Consultant Coordination

Managing collaboration and alignment among architects, engineers, and the broader project team. We ensure all consultants are working toward the same goals on the same timeline.

06

Permitting & Entitlements

Navigating the approvals required from city and county authorities, as well as regulatory processes. We handle the bureaucracy so you don't have to.

07

Cost & Budget Management

Monitoring project expenditures, reviewing change orders, and ensuring financial accountability throughout the project lifecycle. Every dollar is tracked and justified.

08

Schedule Management

Proactive coordination and oversight to keep your project on schedule. We identify delays before they happen and implement recovery strategies when needed.

09

Contractor Coordination

Facilitating effective communication, accountability, and oversight between the owner and the builder at every phase of the project.
